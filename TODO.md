## How the Real-Time Theme Works:
- **Morning (6 AM - 12 PM)**: Warm sunrise colors (cream, orange)
- **Afternoon (12 PM - 6 PM)**: Bright daylight (white, blue accent)
- **Evening (6 PM - 9 PM)**: Sunset colors (purple, gold)
- **Night (9 PM - 6 AM)**: Dark mode (dark gray, green accent)

The theme automatically changes based on your local computer time!
